About Me

Hi there! I'm Michael, currently a first year student attending UC Berkeley. I am a member of the inaugural class in the Management, Entrepreneurship, and Technology Program (MET) here, a joint program between Berkeley's College of Engineering and Haas School of Business. I am pursuing my dual degree in Electrical Engineering Computer Sciences (EECS) and Business Administration.

In my free time, I enjoy hiking, snowboarding, playing tennis, and throwing frisbees. Additionally, I'm love to explore the unexplored and always relish a good adventure. I aspire to apply this same level of curiosity to all my work.

As you peruse through my personal page, make sure to check out some of the past projects I've worked on and connect with me on social media on your way out. If you have any comments, questions, or concerns, drop a message on the Contact page, and I'll be sure to get back to you as soon as possible.
